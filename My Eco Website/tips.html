Simple eci tips to protect our planet

Why This Matters 🌍

Small actions by students can reduce waste, save energy, and protect our future. If everyone does a little, it adds up to a big change.

Click the button to get a tip!

🏠 Tips at Home

  • 💡 Turn off lights when not in use
  • 🥤 Use reusable bottles and containers
  • 🍽️ Reduce food waste
  • 🚿 Take shorter showers
  • ♻️ Recycle properly

🏫 Tips at School

  • 📄 Use both sides of paper
  • 🍱 Bring reusable lunch items
  • 🧹 Pick up litter
  • 💡 Turn off lights when leaving
  • ♻️ Join recycling programs
