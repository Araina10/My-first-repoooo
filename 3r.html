Building A Sustainable Future

Reduce • Reuse • Recycle

♻️ Reduce

What it means

Reduce means using less stuff in the first place so we create less waste.

Examples

- Buy only what you need
- Use less plastic packaging
- Turn off lights and electronics when not needed

🔁 Reuse

What it means

Reuse means using items again instead of throwing them away.

Examples

- Use glass jars for storage
- Reuse shopping bags
- Donate old clothes instead of throwing them away

🔄 Recycle

What it means

Recycle means turning old materials into new products.

Examples

- Recycle paper, plastic, and cans
- Sort waste correctly
- Use recycling bins properly
