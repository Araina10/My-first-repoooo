ECOMENDERS

Learn. Act. Protect.

Welcome to Ecomenders, a website created to help SCC students learn about environmental issues in New Zealand. Here you will find information about climate change, pollution, and waste, as well as the 3R’s – Reduce, Reuse and Recycle. The goal is to promote kaitiakitanga by encouraging students to take small actions to protect the environment.
