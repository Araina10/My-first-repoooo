Environmental Issues in New Zealand

Protecting our land, water, and future

New Zealand is known for its natural beauty, but it still faces serious environmental challenges that affect ecosystems, wildlife, and communities.

🌍Climate Change

What it is

Climate change is the long-term warming of the Earth caused by gases released into the atmosphere.

What's happening in NZ

In New Zealand, climate change is influenced by transport, energy use, and agriculture.

Why it matters

It affects ecosystems, biodiversity, and communities.

💧Water Pollution

What it is

Water pollution occurs when harmful substances contaminate rivers and oceans.

What's happening in NZ

Often caused by agricultural runoff and waste entering waterways.

Why it matters

It harms aquatic life and affects human health.

🌫️Air Pollution

What it is

Air pollution is harmful gases and particles in the air.

What's happening in NZ

Mainly caused by vehicles and burning fuels.

Why it matters

It causes breathing problems and health issues.

♻️Waste and Plastic Pollution

What it is

Plastic pollution is the buildup of waste in the environment.

What's happening in NZ

Large amounts end up in landfills and oceans.

Why it matters

It harms wildlife and ecosystems.
